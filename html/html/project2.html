I decided make my webpage on my favorite tv show, The Office. I have rewatched the show 3 times, and I've recently restarted it again! I consider it to be the perfect comfort show to just have it playing in the background. As humorus as the show pushes to be, you get to watch the characters grow and develop feelings for one another. And I cannot go without mentioning Michael Scott. He is unexplainable, yet unforgettable.

Reasons I like the office:

  • The show is complex, and plot ridden
  • Extremley humourous
  • The redemption arcs in this show are unmatched
