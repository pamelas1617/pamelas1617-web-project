I decided to do my webpage on my favorite tv show the office. I love funny shows and I think the office is super funny. It makes me laugh no matter how many times I've seen it. Its the perfect comfort show to just put on when you're doing anything. Although it is funny, there is plenty of serious moments and character development which I think is what makes it special. I like that its moments where characters learn something feel valuable even if jokes are made simultaneously.

Some of the reasons I like the office include:

  • How complex the characters are
  • How funny the show is
  • The main characters redemption arc
